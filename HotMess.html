HOT MESS

Hot Mess Cosmetics is a fabricated makeup brand that responds to contemporary beauty aesthetics. It challenges the idea of minimal and simplistic beauty trends that have been popular in recent years by embracing bold and expressive makeup looks. Shifting away from the dying “clean girl aesthetic,” my goal was to explore what a makeup line branded towards the artistry of makeup and more dramatic, playful looks would look like.

The product and packaging design use organic, dripping forms that tie into the brand name “Hot Mess.” These forms help to push the idea of self expression through controlled chaos and away from the idea of perfect, minimalistic beauty. I designed the products with a 3D modeling software and then physically produced them through resin. The bright and bold packaging design is intended to compliment the unique forms of the products and the identity of the brand. Overall, this project combines 3D and 2D design as well as commercial beauty branding in order to create a makeup line that focuses on artistic expression rather than conformity.
